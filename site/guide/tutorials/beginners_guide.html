Getting Started

Basic Controls

  • Use the Arrow Keys to move your camera around
    • You can also move the mouse to the edges of the screen to scroll the camera in that direction
  • Left-click to select units, structures, etc.
    • You can also drag the mouse to select multiple units
  • Right-click your target to direct your units
    • Right-clicking on a group of enemy units will direct them towards your
  • After selecting a building from the menu, use Left-click to place it somewhere on the grid.
    • You will need to direct worker units towards the building to construct it. One will work, but the more you bring the faster it will go.

Advanced Features
  • Use CTRL + 0-9 to assign a selected group of units to that number, then use the same number to select that group again
