Project Abstract
The website created for this project will be around a (hypothetial) real-time strategy game.
The game may or may not exist by the time I complete the project, though if it does it will be located on a subpage.

This project will include:
  • On all pages:
    • A header, containing:
      • A title and, potentially, a logo
      • Navigation buttons
  • On documentation-related pages:
    • A page explaining the basic premise of the game (basically the about page)
    • A "getting started" guide for new players
    • Pages for each of the different units, structures, etc.
    • Development history and roadmap
